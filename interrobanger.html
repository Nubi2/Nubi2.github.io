Complextro for charity, for All Nighter Vol. 9

All Nighter is an annual music event where musicians try to make a song within 24 hours, to raise money for charity. From 562 submissions only 45 tracks got selected and put in an album.

Just like last year I worked with Emplexx and Wherefore, along with some new party members: djeb and bad narrator!

Somehow this event happened during my holiday AGAIN, except this year I was well prepared. Using my trusty Thinkpad laptop I was able to work on the song while being abroad in Vancouver.

Concepting

To start the project off I sent a short piano sample to our group chat. Djeb liked the idea and wrote some more music using it. Not long after Emplexx would come in and write a chord progression‚Ä¶ the chain kept going and everything went pretty smoothly!

This was a huge collab project because 5 artists were working on the same song. We needed some kind of structure to keep the song cohesive, so we gave each artist their own task. Emplexx would mainly handle the mixing and arrangement, while Wherefore, djeb and bad narrator would take care of the sound design and brainstorming for example.

Each person would get a specific section of the song assigned to them, so we could all work simultaneously. And so we worked together, sitting in a Discord call, streaming our FL Studio screens.

Written by @emplexx

i mostly worked on the first drop, managing to incorporate the arp sunnexo wrote and vocal chops wherefore made into it. i wanted it to feel reminiscent of classic complextro too, so bits of triplets and saw chords were mandatory.

the drop is 8 bars total divided in half for call and response 4 bars each, which i really wanted to try doing for a while now!

i really love how the two halves contrast in mood between themselves, and also with other parts of the song that surround them‚Ä¶ djeb said that the bass in the second half sounds like a metal sheet being wobbled around which i still think is really funny

in the bridge before and the break after the drop i tried to introduce and tease the lead melody sunnexo also wrote, the one that later plays in full

Written by @djebrayass

it was an honor to work with all these people, the process went super smoothly and altho there are still a few details i would change about the track to make it more cohesive if we had more time, the end result is fucking awesome and i love every single person involved for that

big shoutout to emplexx for handling the main project file and taking care of mixing the whole thing & pretty much arranging it all into a single song :D
